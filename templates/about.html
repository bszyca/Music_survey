Music Survey – about the survey

Fully anonymous, consisting of 23 questions, aims to collect data about ways in which people listen to music. The first seven questions define the experimental group, the remaining questions provide data for analysis.
Estimated time to complete is 3 minutes.

About the project

Analyzing data on how people listen to music – the scope is to develop a web application that will collect answears from respondents about their way of listening to music, collecting responses from the survey placed in the web application and then analysis of the obtained results, concerning the way of listening to music, including examination of the correlation and interrelationships between them. The project is implemented thanks to the cooperation of Radio Gdańsk with students of the Gdańsk University of Technology.
